Help & Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an account? – Go to Sign Up, select "User", and fill in your National ID, name, phone number, and other details.
  • How do I book a parking spot? – After login, choose a zone. If spots are available, select a time to book.
  • What happens if all spots are booked? – You can view when each spot will become available again.

  • How do I check in? – When you log in, the system automatically captures your location and check-in time.
  • How do I process a payment? – On your dashboard, find the user booking and enter the amount + currency (USD or ZiG).
  • How do I cancel a booking? – Use the "Cancel" button next to the user's booking entry.

  • How do I manage marshal shifts? – On the admin dashboard, review each marshal’s shift time and location, and approve or decline it.
  • How do I check amounts collected? – View end-of-day summaries or check shift details to see all transactions processed.
  • Where can I read contact messages? – Contact form submissions appear at the bottom of the admin dashboard.
